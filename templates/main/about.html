About StartUp Incubator

StartUp Incubator provides an integrated dashboard for sharing innovation and startups with success stories across Higher Educational Institutions and relevant stakeholders.


We aim to provide a single platform for students, startup fellows, and entrepreneurs to register and submit their ideas, prototypes, and business plans. We also facilitate various agencies — government and private — to post problem statements, and give venture capitalists and angel investors a space to discover and fund the best ideas.
